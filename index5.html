Miniature Graveyards

The world of building miniature is a world in itself. One person works with trains, another with software used to operate the trains, and yet another paints the panels behind the houses another person builds. Tjalling Boonstra of De Haagse Modelbouwclub said that 'graveyards and scenes of funerals are a very rare exception within miniature building'. In case there is space that needs to be filled up they occasionally think of building a graveyard.

You do find a great batch online.
